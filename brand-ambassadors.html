Brand Ambassadors


We're looking for brand ambassadors! Love candles (or home fragrances) and love to share on social media? You're just who we're looking for!



As a Brand Ambassador you will receive:


  • One complementary fragrance or goody box per quarter;
  • A personalized referral link to share with your friends and followers;
  • Additional loyalty points for all products sold through your referral link.
  • Early access to new scents and products.



In return, you agree to:


  • Post a minimum of six quality referrals per quarter on your social networks (YouTube, blog, Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, etc.);
  • Include the referal link in your post.



FAQ's:


  • Can I choose the contents of my fragrance or goody box? Yes! The last month of the quarter, we will contact you with a list of available scents and goody boxes. You will receive your box the first month of the quarter. No scent or goody box may be repeated. We need your help spreading the word about all we have!
  • What if I there is a problem with the box? Sometimes accidents happen. If your box arrives damaged, we will send a replacement as soon as possible. If you don't like the fragrance, we understand. Not all scents are for everyone. If you have to give a less than positive review, we understand. Please remember, we're here to help us each grow.
  • What counts toward my loyalty points? Everything! Purchases made through your referral link, party sales, and your own purchases!
  • What is considered a quality referral? Let's face it. One tweet is fairly easy to write, but in most cases doesn't have longevity. In return for sending free product, we want your help in spreading the word about our products. So a post on your YouTube channel followed by a tweet, and we're on our way! If you have a YouTube channel or a blog, please tweet the link or share on Facebook and Instagram. If you only are on Twitter, Facebook or Instagram, please write at least one post a month. Whatever you do, please include #indigoscentscandleco and send us a link so we can track and share!
  • I love home fragrances but not candles. Can I swap? Yes, we'll send instructions on how to make a change with your monthly order.



Are you ready? Email us your contact information and include your social media handles. Put "Brand Ambassador" in the subject line.

Email: Indigoscents@gmail.com
