About Us

Indigo Scents is an artisan candle company that was established in 2012 and located in the downtown area of Jackson Tennessee. Indigo Scents is owned by Christal King, a native of Hampton Virginia, and her husband L. Leon King, a Tennessee native and USAF Veteran. Growing up on the East Coast by the ocean and in a town full of fruit trees, flowers, and wild berries, is where Christal developed the love for nature and it's many beautiful aromas! Those aromas are the inspirations behind Indigo Scents.

Indigo Scents specializes in premium scented soy candles and home fragrances. Our scents are made with the hopes of creating the perfect aroma that will evoke a favorite childhood memory or transport you to a tropical getaway from the comforts of your home! Or to simply help you to relax and unwind after a long and busy day. We have scents that will enhance your mood, uplift you, and even energize you!

Our candles are handcrafted in small batches and made with a 100% natural soy wax blend infused with bees wax. What we love about soy wax is that it's sustainable and Eco-friendly. In addition, burning bees wax neutralizes pollutants in the air by releasing negative ions. These negative ions can bind with toxins and help remove them from the air. The fusion of these two natural waxes in our proprietary blend, enables us to produce candles that burn cleaner and cooler than other waxes and will provide you with a candle that is not only highly fragrant, but long lasting!

Lastly, our candles are made with cotton, lead-free wicks, and we use the maximum percentage of premium fragrance oils that smell true to life in our products. Producing strongly scented candles that will fill your home with hours of beautiful aromas and provide the ultimate sensory pleasure! Thanks for choosing Indigo Scents to be a part of your "Ultimate Aromatherapy Experience!"
